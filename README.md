# 🛡️ Virexa Shield

### Digital Wellness & Device Health Platform

Virexa Shield is a modern digital wellness platform designed to help users understand and optimize their device usage behavior through intelligent analytics and actionable insights.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

✔ Detects digital overload patterns  
✔ Tracks app usage & productivity impact  
✔ Identifies privacy risks (non-intrusive insights)  
✔ Generates a smart device optimization report  
✔ Improves overall digital wellbeing score  

---

## 🧠 Problem It Solves

In today's digital world, users often struggle with:
- Excessive screen time
- Poor app usage habits
- Hidden privacy risks
- Lack of actionable insights

Virexa Shield transforms raw usage data into meaningful insights that help users take control of their digital lifestyle.

---

## 💡 Solution

The platform provides:
- 📊 Usage Intelligence Dashboard
- 🔋 Device Health Monitoring
- 🔐 Privacy Awareness Insights
- 📈 Smart Optimization Reports
